Introduction

Have you ever stopped to think how much we consume? Since perfumes, beauty products, clothes, or, most vital things like food and water. But we always forget to count what we spend most, the energy.

Energy is a vital matter for modern everyday life, it is in things like computers, refrigerators, showers and even in the middle that you, dear reader, are using to have access to this site. And knowing the enormous amount of energy we spend, even so there is a disregard for her economy, because it is much easier to see someone practicing recycling than saving energy.

But even so, this is only a portion of the problem, because, saving little and spending a lot, it will be necessary to produce an excess of energy, and it is then that the problem begins.

This can affect the planet in many ways, from destroying the environment to building hydroelectric plants, to releasing toxic gases into the atmosphere, thus helping the greenhouse effect. There are still other examples, such as the improper disposal of nuclear material.

Knowing all this now you may be curious about how this all works, coming down the screen a little you can understand much more about it.
